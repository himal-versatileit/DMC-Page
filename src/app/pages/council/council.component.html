SHRI ASPI ERACH DAMANIA

President

Roles and Responsibilities:

To preside over all the meetings of the council & regulate the conduct of business at such meetings. Watch over the financial and executive administration of the council. Exercise supervision and control over the act and proceedings of all the officers and servants of the council in matter of executive administration and in matters concerning the accounts and records of council. In cases of emergency direct the execution or stoppage of any work or the doing of any act which requires the sanction of council.

MEET OUR COUNCIL

DR. SANJAM SINGH

Chief Officer

Roles and Responsibilities:

Subject to the control, direction and supervision of the president. Supervise the financial and executive administration of the council. To take step to give effect to all the decisions of the resolution of council / standing committee. To maintain and supervise the accounts and register of the council. To take step to remove any irregularity pointed out by the municipal auditor. Prepare budget estimates and submit to standing committee. Report to president and the committee concern all cases of fraud embezzlement, theft, loss of municipal money and property. Exercise supervision and control over the acts and proceedings of all the officers and servants of the council.
