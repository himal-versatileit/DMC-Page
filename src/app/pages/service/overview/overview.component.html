Overview DMC

In order to bring transparency and smooth collaboration within various departments of Daman Municipal council and for the benefit of the citizens Daman Municipal Council has embraced E-Governance and facilitating most essential citizen services through this Portal. The portal offers information about various aspects of the city, its governance and facilitates citizen services such as Water tax, Property tax, Registration of complaints, Grievance etc. Initially few services will be offered on this portal, other services will be offered subsequently in a phased manner. On this portal, Citizens can file online complaints and subsequently track the status of their complaints, Grievance. Citizen can apply online for new water connection and Permissions for building and for assessment for the property. It is also possible to pay property and water taxes through electronic clearance system.
