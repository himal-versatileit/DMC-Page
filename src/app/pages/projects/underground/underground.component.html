Daman Underground Sewerage Project

Daman Municipal Council has provided a facility to the citizen of Daman by coming up with this major project of Sewerage networking and Sewerage Treatment plant for Moti Daman and Nani Daman with gravity and vacuum sewerage system.

  • Sewerage treatment plant situated at Rajiv Gandhi Setu has the capacity of 4.21MLD based on Sequential Batch Reactor Technology.
  • Sewerage networking with gravity line is about 18km in Moti Daman Urban area.
  • Also covering Mitnawad and Kharawad about 8km (part) with Vacuum networking system in Nani Daman.
